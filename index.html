About Me

Hey! I'm Moritz, a producer, mixer, and mastering engineer living in Berlin. I've been making music for most of my life, but pursued it professionally in 2017 when I moved to Berlin to study at the Abbey Road Institute. Since graduating in 2019, I've been producing, mixing and mastering for many artists while also releasing music myself. As 'mosqido' I make music as a creative outlet, to have fun and push my own boundaries. Whether i'm making an indie-pop record and singing myself or producing a techno/dance track for the club, I always try to challenge myself and learn something new from each project. Experimentation and staying open-minded are part of my core values. Many of my favorite sounds and sections happened because of happy accidents and, sometimes, hours of trial and error. While collaborating, I value creating a space of trust where there is no judgment, so we can focus on what's important: Creating and having fun. *a never-ending quest for learning more about all things sound.*
